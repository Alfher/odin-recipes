Lasagna

Descritption

How to make Lasagna like your Italian nonna does, haha.

Ingredients

  • pasta sheets
  • cheese
  • vegetables
  • pasata (tomato sauce)
  • love
  1. boil water
  2. add pasta sheets (boil for 10 mins)
  3. prepare vegetables (with love)
  4. put all ingredients together in a layered manner (with lots of love) and bake in oven for 20 mins
  5. remove from oven, add cheese, and let it cool down a little before slicing up and serving. Et voila!
  6. eat
